How to Withdraw Funds from Treasure NFT? (Step-by-Step Guide) 💰

If you're investing in Treasure NFT, knowing how to withdraw your funds safely is essential. Whether you're using BEP-20 or TRC-20, this guide will walk you through the entire withdrawal process in a simple and secure way. 🚀

Follow these step-by-step instructions to withdraw your earnings smoothly! ✅

Step 1: Open the Treasure NFT App 📲

First, launch the Treasure NFT app on your mobile device and go to the "Assets" section. This is where you'll find your current balance and available options for withdrawing funds.

Step 2: Select the "Withdraw" Option 💰

Click on "Withdraw" to view the available withdrawal methods.
You'll see two blockchain options:

  • 🔹 BEP-20 (Binance Smart Chain)
  • 🔹 TRC-20 (Tron Network)

Choose the one that suits your crypto wallet or preferred exchange.

💡 Tip: BEP-20 is commonly used for Binance-based wallets, while TRC-20 is ideal for Tron wallets.

Step 3: Choose Your Blockchain Network 🔄

Before proceeding, select the correct blockchain (BEP-20 or TRC-20). If you send funds to an incompatible network, you may lose your withdrawal permanently.

👉 Double-check your network to avoid mistakes!

Step 4: Enter Your Wallet Address 🏦

Copy your wallet address from your preferred exchange or crypto wallet (Trust Wallet, Binance, Bybit, or CoinDCX).
Paste it into the withdrawal section of the Treasure NFT app.
Confirm that the address matches the correct blockchain.

🚨 Warning: Entering the wrong wallet address can result in permanent loss of funds!

Step 5: Input the Withdrawal Amount 📝

Type in the amount you want to withdraw.
If you want to withdraw your full balance, simply click "All Withdraw."

  • ✅ Minimum Withdrawal Limit: $50
  • ⚠️ Transaction Fee: A small fee applies based on the selected blockchain

Step 6: Verify Your Withdrawal with OTP 🔐

Open Google Authenticator on your phone.
Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) displayed in the app.
This step ensures security and prevents unauthorized withdrawals.

🔒 Never share your OTP with anyone!

Step 7: Submit & Confirm Your Withdrawal ✅

Click "Submit" to send your withdrawal request.
A confirmation screen will appear—click "Confirm" to finalize.
At this point, your withdrawal is successfully requested and processing begins.

Step 8: Wait for Processing ⏳ (Up to 96 Hours)

Withdrawals typically take up to 96 hours to process.
During this time, Treasure NFT reviews and approves the transaction.
You can check the withdrawal status anytime in the "Assets" section".

📌 Be patient! If you don't receive funds within 96 hours, contact Treasure NFT support.

Supported Wallets for Withdrawals 💼

You can withdraw your Treasure NFT funds to any of the following wallets without issues:

  • ✅ Trust Wallet
  • ✅ Binance
  • ✅ Bybit
  • ✅ CoinDCX
💡 Tip: Always use a secure and trusted wallet for your crypto transactions.

Final Thoughts: Secure & Easy Withdrawals 🎯

Withdrawing from Treasure NFT is a safe and straightforward process if you follow these steps carefully. Make sure you:

  • ✅ Select the correct blockchain (BEP-20 or TRC-20)
  • ✅ Enter the right wallet address
  • ✅ Verify with OTP for security
  • ✅ Wait up to 96 hours for processing

Now that you know how to withdraw your funds, you can enjoy your earnings hassle-free! 🚀💸
