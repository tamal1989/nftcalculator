❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💡 How does the dual earning work?

On the final day of any stacking period, your funds earn both the stacking rate and the reservation system rate simultaneously for compound growth.

⏳ What happens after stacking period ends?

Funds automatically return to the reservation system and qualify for higher stacking zones when balance thresholds are met.

💸 Can I withdraw promotional balance?

The $150 promotional credit expires after 3 days if no additional investment is made. Maintain your investment to keep the bonus.

🔄 How often can I stack?

You can enter stacking zones whenever your balance meets the threshold. Each stacking cycle lasts 3 days with automatic renewal options.
